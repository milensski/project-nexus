How it Works

1. Project Listings

Students, industry partners, and nonprofits can create project listings detailing the scope, objectives, requirements, and expected deliverables of their projects.

2. Browse and Apply

Students can browse through project listings based on their interests, skills, and career goals. They can read project descriptions, view project requirements, and apply to participate in projects that align with their interests and expertise.

3. Project Teams

Once selected for a project, students are assigned to project teams led by a project manager or mentor. Project teams typically consist of a mix of students with different skills and backgrounds to encourage collaboration and diversity of perspectives.
